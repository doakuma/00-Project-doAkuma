A. Road Traffic Environment and Automobile Culture

(1) Change of the Road Traffic Environment

The recent road traffic environment of Korea has changed in several aspects, particular noticeable are the drastic increase of the elderly population of 65 years or older of age, increase of women drivers, and change of automobile and road space and others. The number of registered vehicles in Korea has been drastically increased from the later part of 1980s and exceeded 20 million cars in 2014. The number of cars per 100 persons and the length of road extension are being increased, however, with the drastic increase of cars, the road space per car is being moderately declined.

While the entire population showed the rate of increase for 1.0% of annual average, the rate of increase in annual average for elderly population reached 4.3%.

(2) Desirable automobile culture

While there is a drastic increase of cars, you come across the new vocabularies such traffic culture or automobile culture. However, in reality not only we are unable to free ourselves from poor traffic environment but also desirable car culture has yet to be established. From complying with basic parking etiquette in the limited parking space to avoid even the tiniest damage to others, obey the stop line on the cross street, wearing seatbelt, etc. would be the barometer of that nation’s traffic culture.

Advanced traffic culture prioritize in safety rather than traffic flow, person over cars. Even with the advanced traffic system and complete systematic devices, without the proper conception of traffic culture by a driver or a pedestrian, it will not be as effective.

  • • Increase of Car versus car accident

    In 2014, the Car versus car accidents reach 72.5% among entire accidents. In order to prevent the accidents between the automobiles, active communication is needed such as turn on the signal while driving.

  • • icon Increase of weekend accident

    There has been increase on friday and weekend traffic accident with more people enjoying leisure activities due to expansion of 5 working days a week in work places. To prevent the traffic accidents, make sure you get plenty of rest before going on the road for long/short distance driving, conduct prior inspection of the car and drive safely with relaxed driving plan.

  • • Increase of nighttime accident

    Recently, with the growing number of nighttime activities, accidents occur frequently in the late night from 10:00 P.M. to early morning hours. To reduce the nighttime accident, you should let other drivers know your movements by turning on the headlight as it starts to get dusky outside and turn on the signal before you change your driving directions and make sure you have smooth communication with other drivers. For nighttime driving, extra attention is required than the day time for safe driving.

(2) Why do traffic accidents occur?

  • • icon By the unsafe traffic environment

    Unsafe traffic environment means situations given to the driver externally such as decline of vision due to darkness, slippery surface, lack of traffic safety facilities, defective vehicle maintenance, sudden cutting in of cars to the front and others. particularly, most common to witness on the road where the regulations is frequently violated is either the cars or pedestrians who are violating the regulations.

  • • icon Due to the lack of accurate knowledge and ambiguous driving behavior

    Many traffic accidents are caused by the lack of knowledge. For example, when speeding or braking under the special situation of snowy road, rainy road or curved road, it is unable respond because of the unknown conditions of the cars.

[When the special distinction of snowy road is unknown it can lead to an accident]

  • • Lack of recognition of danger

    Under the same circumstances, some drivers feel danger and take action, by slowing down, and some drivers don’t. This depends on the difference in experiences as “Dangerous”or “Not so dangerous” but it is because the standard of dhow much is considered as dangerous. The stronger your desire to be your destination, the more risk you take.

  • • icon Due to the wrong driving attitude

    The elements of dangerous driving are the driving attitude of not complying the traffic laws and regulations with the mentality of ‘no traffic accident, no problem’, impetuous attitude whenever sitting behind the wheel even when you are running late, selfish attitude without the consideration for other drivers or pedestrians, tendency of changing attitude at will and easily get excited, over-confidence of driving skill and attempt to show it and others.

  • • icon Due to the incorrect driving habit

    While dangerous driving continues and accidents do not occur, you start to believe that “These conducts are not dangerous”and this very belief is “Unawareness of safety”. Inappropriate driving habits start from such belief.

[Rushing predicts danger at all times]

(3) Measures to prevent traffic accident

  • • icon Let people notice you

    The modern society will not notice you if you do not inform yourself to them. Same applies to driving. It is important to show others your driving and inform them of your car movement. That is a way for others to help you or avoid the danger from you.

  • • icon Compliance with traffic laws and regulations is the safety device for your family

    Everyone expects for pedestrian and car to comply with the traffic laws and regulations. However, the main cause of unsafe driving is to create the unexpected situations. Even if the other drivers violate the traffic laws and regulations, the accident may not occur if you keep the rules.

  • • icon All of sudden maneuvering are red light for safe driving

    It would be very difficult to respond when other drivers cut in or the car in front applies the sudden brake, particularly the driver is looking elsewhere. Act of sudden changes in short period of time, such as sudden handling maneuver, sudden braking, sudden starting, sudden entry and others raises the red signal for safe driving.

[Compliance with traffic laws and regulations is the safety device for all my family members.]

  • •Safe driving starts with maintaining appropriate speed and safe distance

    There are times when you have sufficient distance with a car approaching from far back but due to much faster speed than expected it end up in accident. It is not easy to determined the speed of a car approaching from far behind thus you should not judge whether it is “Dangerous”or “Not dangerous”. Remember that it is beginning of safe driving when you maintain proper speed posted by law and within the capability range of driver which is yourself.

  • •Active information exchange is the green light for accident prevention

    Positive communication while driving takes important role in preventing accident. When a car in front attempts left turn at the intersection and suddenly slows down and stop, and the car behind attempts to pass by making 180 degree u-turn will result in accident.

    In this situation, had the car in the front indicated intention on u-turn, the car behind would not have attempted to pass, and even the car behind was in the no passing zone had it showed signal to pass, the car in front could have responded. This is why the efforts should be made for positive communication as if the car next to you or in front of you is a family or a close friend.

[Inform other drivers with the signal before the lane change.]

[Maintaining appropriate distance looks right and is safe]

[Accident may occur when there is no communication]

  • • Develop the risk anticipating skills

    You need to always have the idea in your mind that other cars or pedestrians might violate traffic regulation. In other words consider that the movements of other drivers may be disadvantage to you and prepare for the situation. Perhaps while you practice this ability, your risk anticipation would improve.

  • •Drive in calm and cool manner

    Among the most common reason for traffic violation is time advantage in other words, to reduce the driving time. However you might want to think how much could that time advantage can help you. To save few minutes, not only you are risking the citation by the police and suspension of your driver’s license but also you are risking the danger of traffic accident.

[Controlled driving is a smallinvestment for accident prevention]

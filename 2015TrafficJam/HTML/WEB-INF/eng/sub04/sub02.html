History

2000's history

  • 01/01/2011 Consolidated Road Traffic Authority and DRIVER'S LICENSE AGENCY
  • 06/22/2008 Changed title to the [Road Traffic Authority]
  • 12/01/2005 Opened the Korea Traffic Broadcasting Station
  • 10/31/2005 Changed title Traffic Broadcasting Station [Korea Traffic Broadcasting Station]
  • 05/15/2002 Opened the Jeonju Traffic Broadcasting Station
  • 11/30/2001 Opened the Incheon Traffic Broadcasting Station
  • 11/13/2001 Opened the Gangwon Traffic Broadcasting Station

1990's history

  • 07/30/1999 Opened the Daegu Traffic Broadcasting Station
  • 07/14/1999 Opened the Daejeon Traffic Broadcasting Station
  • 12/23/1997 Opened the Busan Traffic Broadcasting Station
  • 12/20/1997 Opened the Gwangju Traffic Broadcasting Station

1980's history

  • 01/17/1986 Became a regular member of the Transportation Research Board of America(TRB)
  • 05/19/1981 Became a regular member of Prevention Routiere Internationale(PRI)
  • 05/22/1980 Changed title to the [Road Traffic Safety Association]

1950's history

  • 02/12/1954 Established [Central Union of Korea Traffic Safety Association]

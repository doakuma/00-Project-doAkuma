About Us Major Prgram

Major Prgram

Technical Support for Traffic Safety

  • •Improving traffic in frequent accident areas and providing statistical analysis
  • •Technical support for traffic accident investigations
  • •Suggestions for the design, supervision, inspection, and improvement of traffic safety facilities
  • •Improvement of the 'Safety Zone'
  • •Technical support, design, and supervision for traffic signal systems
  • •Management and inspection of unattended traffic enforcement equipment on consignment

Public Relations for Traffic Safety

  • • National public relations campaigns aimed at enhancing the national understanding of traffic order principles and preventing traffic accidents
  • •Pan-national campaign, 'Reduce Traffic Accidents'... photographic exhibition... composition competition... other related campaigns
  • • Public relations campaigns through TV, radio, newspaper, magazine, slogans, and ad poles
  • •Children's Traffic Safety Public Relations Center: A free and experiential training ground for traffic safety training in early years

Traffic Safety Training

  • •Driver training: Training of traffic violators in traffic laws and regulations, basic traffic information training, participatory training at the traffic site, traffic safety training for applicants of driver's licenses
  • •Social traffic safety training: For students and teachers of schools in all levels, social organizations, and companies
  • •Traffic professionals training: Police officers in charge of traffic accident investigation

Traffic Broadcasting Network(TBN)

  • • Provides fast and accurate traffic information to prevent traffic congestion and accidents
  • •Contributes to the establishment of a new traffic culture, based on continuous public relations activities in traffic safety and order
  • • Provides a variety of information for the convenience of listeners

Research of Traffic Science

  • •The operation of international certified test agency(KOLAS)
  • •R&D of testing and specifications for safety facilities
  • •R&D of traffic equipment and traffic information management systems using (ITS)
  • •The evaluation and standardization of function and technology
  • •Research of traffic control and management methods
  • •The development and standardization of traffic signal control systems
  • •The development of road safety policies involved with the training of traffic safety related laws and regulations, and the operation of a driving behavior and vehicle simulator
  • •The introduction and development of traffic safety technologies through international exchange, and an industry-academy-research institute technological exchange for the enhancement of international research competencies
